Ms. Vera Chidera

Founder & CEO

Leading With Purpose

Ms. Vera Chidera is an entrepreneur and environmental advocate driven by a deep commitment to women's health and sustainability. Her journey with Green AVIC represents the intersection of environmental conservation, social entrepreneurship, and women's empowerment.

Her work began after witnessing the environmental damage caused by plastic waste and the lack of safe menstrual care options for women. This experience became the catalyst for creating Green AVIC, a brand that addresses both environmental and health challenges through plant-based menstrual products.

Through Green AVIC, Ms. Vera is working to provide accessible menstrual care solutions while promoting education, awareness, and environmental stewardship. Her vision extends beyond products to building sustainable communities where women's health and environmental care go hand in hand.

Three Pillars of Leadership

Ms. Vera's work spans environmental advocacy, women's health, and social entrepreneurship

Environmental Advocate

Ms. Vera leads initiatives to reduce plastic waste and promote sustainable practices. Her 2022 community plastic cleanup earned recognition from the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network, highlighting her commitment to environmental conservation and community education.

Women's Health Champion

Dedicated to empowering women through safe, accessible menstrual care, Ms. Vera advocates for breaking stigmas around women's health. She promotes education and awareness, ensuring women have the knowledge and resources to make informed choices about their bodies.

Social Entrepreneur

As founder of Green AVIC, Ms. Vera demonstrates how business can be a force for good. She builds sustainable communities by creating products that support both women's health and environmental protection, proving that profit and purpose can go hand in hand.

Vision for the Future

Ms. Vera envisions a world where every woman has access to safe, affordable menstrual care without compromising the health of our planet. Through Green AVIC, she is building a movement that:

  • ✓ Reduces plastic waste through biodegradable menstrual products
  • ✓ Educates communities about sustainable living and women's health
  • ✓ Empowers women entrepreneurs and advocates
  • ✓ Creates sustainable economic opportunities in local communities
