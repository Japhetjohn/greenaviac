Where It All Began

Green AVIC was born from an experience.

In 2022, our founder led a community plastic cleanup that revealed the true scale of damage caused by synthetic waste on the environment. Seeing how plastic pollution affects both the earth and women's health became the foundation of Green AVIC.

The cleanup initiative brought together community members, young people, and environmental advocates to address the growing problem of plastic waste in our local environment. What started as a single event became a catalyst for lasting change.

Today, that experience continues to guide everything we do from the products we create to the communities we serve.

This work earned recognition from the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network for its contribution to community education and environmental conservation.
