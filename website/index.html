PAST IMPACT

Making a difference, one step at a time

In 2022, Green AVIC founder Ms. Vera and her team organized a community-level plastic sanitation exercise to help curb the negative impact of synthetic material pollution on the environment.

The realization of how much damage plastic dumps cause to the Earth added to her zeal for founding Green AVIC - for a healthier female population and livable Earth. She sensitized young boys and girls on key aspects of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

MEET THE FOUNDER

The visionary behind Green AVIC's mission

Founder & CEO

Ms. Vera

Championing Women's Health & Environmental Sustainability

Ms. Vera is a visionary entrepreneur and environmental advocate dedicated to transforming menstrual health care in Nigeria and across Africa. Her passion for creating sustainable solutions was ignited by witnessing the devastating impact of plastic waste on communities and the environment.

In 2022, she led a groundbreaking community-level plastic sanitation exercise that earned recognition from the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network, receiving a Certificate of Excellence for her innovative approach to environmental conservation and community education.

Through Green AVIC, Ms. Vera is on a mission to equip women and girls with safe, plant-based menstrual care products while empowering them through education and advocacy. Her work focuses on reducing stigma, promoting sustainability, and ensuring that quality menstrual health care is accessible to all women, regardless of their location or economic status.
