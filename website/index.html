About Green Avic

Green AVIC was created from a simple but powerful realization…the way we care for women's bodies should not harm the planet.

Witnessing the growing damage caused by plastic waste and the lack of safe menstrual options inspired the birth of Green AVIC.

Today, we create plant-based sanitary products and support education and advocacy that help women live healthier, more confident lives.

Where It All Began

Green AVIC was born from a powerful experience in 2022… a community plastic cleanup that revealed the true scale of environmental damage and its impact on women's health.

What started as one initiative grew into a mission recognized by the United Nations for its contribution to sustainable development and community education.

Meet The Founder

Founder & CEO

Ms. Vera Chidera: Founder & CEO

Ms. Vera Chidera is an entrepreneur and environmental advocate driven by a deep commitment to women's health and sustainability.

Her journey began after witnessing the environmental damage caused by plastic waste and the lack of safe menstrual care options for women. Through Green AVIC, she is working to provide plant-based menstrual products while promoting education, awareness and access for women everywhere.
